How has technology affected my daily life

May 26, 2021
Sampanna Pokharel

I was born in early 2000s so I somewhat grew up alongside the era of technology and internet. I was first introduced to computers in my pre teens when my parents first bought a personal computer for their office work and I was absolutely fascinated by it. A small squared box which was sitting at the corner of our bedroom was packed with so many things and had so many different features, that it was probabily the first time (as far as I remember) that I was overwhelmed with joy and curiosity. When I first experienced it, I felt that it was like a new television. Are you listening to music ? don't like current song ? you can just skip it. Same with videos, you could control what you want to watch and when you want to watch it. This experience was my first proper interaction with technology.

That small fasciniation with computers during my pre teens has led me in present time where I spend most of my time in front of a computer; surrounded by technology. I am heavily dependent on technologies, so much so that if I were to seperate myself from technology for a day, none of my tasks that I had planned for that day would progress or move forward and my day would not be productive. We as a human being have many requirements and needs, two of which are entertainment and socialization. As technology has strongly been embedded in my life, I entertain myself and socialize with people through technology. Technology has made such an impact in my life that even the decision to choose a college for my undergrad study was heavily influenced by the people I met on an online game. If I didn't have met those people, then right now I would have been walking on a completely different path.

Constantly being surrounded by technology also has its own drawbacks. I as a student feel that technology has done more harm than good in terms of my academics. Sure, I get to access information with ease and learn new topics and communicate with my teachers and friends, but I constantly feel distracted. Previously, I had better focus and could give my full undivided attention towards a specific task whether it be reading books or making notes. But now in present, I find it difficult to focus on a single task for a long period of time without getting distracted. Since the task that previously required me to move around a lot and peform different physicsal activities has now been replaced by technology (socializing), I now move a lot less and spend most of my time indoors which has had an affect on my health (both mental and physical) to some extent.

Good and bad effects of technology in my life is shown in the following table:

Positive Negative
Easy communication between teachers, family members and friends less physical interraction with others
Easy to research on a topic using Internet little to no exposure on other means of research and getting information
Socialize with people, learn new topics, get entertainment and peform other activities from a single device Too much screen time has let me to having poor eyesight

What is Database

May 26, 2021
Sampanna Pokharel

Database is a collection of organised data and information in a computer system which is used for data management (Hughes, 2019) .Databases are made up of several tables which represent real world entities. Tables store data in organized manner in forms of rows and columns. In a table, rows represent each records and column represents attributes of those records. By storing data using database, we can easily access, manage, modify, update, control and organize them. Structured Query language (SQL) is used to perform actions such as delete, update ,add, modify and to interact with data of a database.

Before databases, people worked on spreadsheets to manage their data. The downside of spreadsheet was that it did not support multi user functionality and could not perform complex data manipulations. Database however could do things that spreadsheet could not; it could perform complicated data manipulations, provided database access to multiple users at a same time, and provided better data security. (Oracle, 2020) .

Databases are managed using Database Management Software (DBMS). They have the following features:

  • Multi-User support
  • Maintains data integrity
  • Enhanced data security
  • Provides data backup and recovery
  • Prevents data redundancy
